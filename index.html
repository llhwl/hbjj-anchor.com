About Huaibei Jinjiu New Materials Co., Ltd.

Founded in 2004, Huaibei Jinjiu New Materials Co., Ltd. is a professional manufacturer of resin anchoring agents, FRP anchor rods, MG resin and mining support materials based in Anhui, China.

With a 16,000+ sqm factory, 3 fully automated production lines, and over 100 employees, we serve mining, tunneling and construction clients across 30+ countries including Russia, Turkey, Indonesia, South Africa, Chile, Vietnam, Peru, Poland, and Brazil.

Our products are used in coal mine roadway bolting, tunnel support, slope stabilization, foundation anchoring, and civil engineering projects worldwide.
